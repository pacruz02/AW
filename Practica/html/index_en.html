Sustainable Mobility Platform

The electric vehicle fleet management web platform aims to provide a complete business solution for managing and sharing these resources. It allows you to control the reservation and return of vehicles, manage users with different roles (employees and administrators), and ensure an efficient and sustainable use of corporate mobility. Additionally, it includes accessibility options for inclusive use and statistics modules that facilitate strategic decision-making.

Benefits of Electric Vehicles

  1. Zero direct emissions: They do not produce harmful gases while driving, helping to reduce air pollution in cities.
  2. Lower operating costs: They consume less "fuel" (electricity vs gasoline) and require less maintenance (no oil or many moving parts).
  3. Silent and efficient driving: They offer a smoother driving experience, with immediate acceleration and less noise.

Main Services

  • Vehicle reservation and return processes, with autonomy and availability checks.
  • User management with differentiated roles (employees and administrators).
  • Dealer management
  • Vehicle management
  • Reservation management and history by user.
